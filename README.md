# 🚀 Learning Path at NIT Academy

NIT Academy is focused on building capacity in our students through a **real-time job immersion culture**. We don't just teach theory; we walk you through the inner workings of the IT industry to prepare you for high-impact roles.

The IT landscape is vast, but our curriculum is designed to provide a deep, practical understanding of the core components and technologies required to build a successful business or excel at a top-tier IT company.

## 🎓 What You Will Learn

### 🐧 The Linux Ecosystem
Master the foundation of the modern internet. You will dive deep into Enterprise Linux distributions, learning system administration, kernel optimization, and command-line mastery.

### ♾️ DevOps (Developmental Operations)
Learn the culture and tools that bridge the gap between development and operations. We cover CI/CD pipelines, automation, and the modern workflows used by global engineering teams.

### 🛠️ Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)
Understand how to design, build, and maintain scalable and highly reliable software systems. You will learn the principles of monitoring, incident response, and capacity planning.

### 📱 Full-Stack Architecture
Ever wondered how web and mobile apps actually work "under the hood"? We break down the **Technology Stack**, from frontend interfaces to backend logic and database management.

### 🏢 Data Center Infrastructure
Get hands-on experience with our **Physical Mini-Data Center**. Unlike programs that only use the cloud, you will learn the hardware, virtualization, and networking that power the digital world.

### 🛡️ Network & Security
Learn how to secure infrastructure and optimize performance using modern industry tools for remote access and traffic management.
